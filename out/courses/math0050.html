Math 0050

This class is a slower-paced introductory mathematics course intended and is recommended for non STEM-concentrators or students with less background in math who want a slow paced introduction to calculus or a more thorough foundation in algebra. It cannot be taken in combination to MATH 0070 or MATH 0090. Topics include, but are not limited to functions, graphing functions, limits, an intro to differentiation and integration, and the mean value theorem.
